# Project Naming Brainstorm

## Top Contenders ("Cuddle" Riffs)

1.  **Huddle**
    *   *Why:* Rhymes with "Cuddle". Implies bringing elements together (nodes) or a team gathering.
    *   *Tagline:* "Huddle your views together."
    *   *Extension:* `.hdl` or `.hud`

2.  **Snug**
    *   *Why:* Related to cuddling ("snuggle"). Implies a tight, safe fit (type-safety).
    *   *Tagline:* "Type-safe and snug templates for Go."
    *   *Extension:* `.snug`

3.  **Coze**
    *   *Why:* Short for "Cozy". Sounds warm and comfortable.
    *   *Tagline:* "Write cozy code."
    *   *Extension:* `.cz` or `.coze`

4.  **Nuzzle**
    *   *Why:* Direct synonym for cuddle.
    *   *Tagline:* "Nuzzle your logic close to your view."
    *   *Extension:* `.nuz`

## Technical / Acronyms

5.  **KDML** (User Suggestion)
    *   *Why:* "KDL Markup Language". Pronounced "Cuddle-Em-El". Very descriptive.
    *   *Extension:* `.kdml`

6.  **Kodl**
    *   *Why:* Portmanteau of **Ko** (Go) + **Kdl**. Sounds distinct.
    *   *Extension:* `.kdl` (native) or `.kodl`

7.  **Kaddle**
    *   *Why:* Phonetic spelling of KDL, making the pronunciation explicit.
    *   *Extension:* `.kdl`

## Abstract

8.  **Embrace**
    *   *Why:* Wrapping your HTML in a type-safe embrace.
    *   *Extension:* `.emb`

9.  **Nest**
    *   *Why:* KDL is all about nesting nodes. (Note: Possible collision with NestJS).
    *   *Extension:* `.nst`
